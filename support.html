Support Us

Join Us in Making a Difference

Ways You Can Help

At Schools Without Borders, we rely on the generous support of individuals, communities, and organizations to continue our vital work. There are many ways you can contribute to our mission of bringing education to children in crisis-affected areas.

Your support allows us to maintain our mobile schools, purchase educational materials, provide food for students, and ensure that our teachers and staff are trained and equipped to deliver quality education.

How You Can Contribute

Volunteer With Us

Your time and expertise can have a direct impact on the lives of children in Gaza. Whether you're an educator, healthcare worker, or have skills in logistics, administration, or any other field, we welcome volunteers who share our commitment to making a difference.

As a volunteer, you can assist in organizing educational programs, provide psychological support, offer training to teachers, or engage in fundraising activities.

Become a Volunteer

Donate

Your financial contributions directly support the children we serve. Donations help us maintain our mobile schools, purchase educational materials, provide food for students, and ensure that our teachers and staff are trained and equipped to deliver quality education.

You can make a one-time donation or set up a monthly contribution to ensure that our programs continue to thrive.

Make a Donation

Spread the Word

One of the simplest ways to support us is to help spread awareness about our mission. Share our story on social media, tell your friends and family about our work, and encourage others to get involved.

The more people who know about our efforts, the more children we can reach with education and hope.

Partnerships

If you're part of a company or organization, consider partnering with Schools Without Borders to help us expand our impact. Your support can take the form of sponsorships, donations, or offering resources that directly benefit our programs.

Corporate partnerships allow us to continue creating sustainable solutions for education in crisis areas.

The Impact of Your Support

Every contribution to Schools Without Borders directly impacts the lives of children affected by conflict. Your support doesn't just provide education—it offers hope, stability, and the chance for a better future.

When you donate or volunteer with us, you become part of a movement that believes in the power of education to transform lives and rebuild communities.

Restore Learning Opportunities

Your support helps children regain access to education despite challenging circumstances.

Provide Safe Spaces

Your contributions create secure environments where children can learn, play, and grow.

Support Teacher Training

Your generosity helps us train educators to deliver quality education in crisis situations.

Foster Psychological Healing

Your donation supports programs that address trauma and promote emotional wellbeing.
